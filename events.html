How to Get to HKUST

  • By MTR: Take the MTR Tseung Kwan O Line and get off at Hang Hau Station (Exit B1). Then take Minibus 11M or 11 to HKUST.
  • By Bus: From Diamond Hill MTR, take Bus 91 to Clear Water Bay (get off at the HKUST South Entrance stop) or Bus 91M to Po Lam (get off at the HKUST North Entrance stop).
  • By Minibus: Take Minibus 11, 11M between Hang Hau MTR (Exit B1) and HKUST, or Minibus 12 from Po Lam to HKUST.
The Minibus terminal is next to the HKUST South Entrance; all buses/minibuses stop there.
For further details, see: HKUST Official Directions.

  • By Taxi: Take a red taxi (urban taxi) to "Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Clear Water Bay".
  • Estimated Fare & Time: The journey from Kowloon or Hong Kong Island generally costs HKD 120-180 and takes 20-45 minutes depending on traffic.
  • Drop-off: Taxis can drop you off at the Main Entrance (outside Academic Building) or South Entrance.
Show the driver: 香港科技大學（清水灣）

  • By Car: Input "Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Clear Water Bay" or use Clear Water Bay Road in your navigation system.
  • Parking: Visitor parking is available (charged hourly, Octopus only) at the Main Entrance car park.
  • Note: Parking spaces may be limited during large events.
For more info, see: HKUST Parking Info
